Society Leader

The current leader of the Extreme Sports Society is Bradley Martyn. Bradley is one of the very first members of the society and has helped with its growth and development eversince. He participates in both wingsuit flying and snowboarding. He is a real adrenaline chaser! If you are thinking of joining our awesome society and have any questions about it Bradley is the best person to contact. He will make sure all your questions are clearly answered and help you with whatever you need, even help you decide which extreme sport to pick! You can contact him by messaging the society's Facebook or Instagram pages.
