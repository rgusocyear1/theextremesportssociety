What is a wingsuit?


A wingsuit in short is a custom suit made for skydivers who arenâ€™t happy with the traditional method of plummeting thousands of feet in the quickest time possible, apparently that was not thrilling enough for some. Wingsuits are made from a variety of materials, the main surface is typically made from ripstop nylon, with various materials used to reinforce the leading edge, and reduce drag. The suit itself closely resembles the genetic design of flying squirrels. Wingsuits are piloted by shoulder movement, knees, and hips and requires a high level of muscular fitness. Piloting a wingsuit is an extremely complex operation, therefore, pilots are required to have completed a minimum of 200 base jumps before strapping one on.
